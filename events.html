Detroit is home to many events and activities. With so many choices there is something for everyone. Home to the Detroit Tigers, Comerica Park is a great place to visit on a trip to Detroit. If you don't like sports, visitors might also enjoy the Sound Broad inside Motor City Casino or The Fox Theatre. Both of these places host a variety of shows. You're guaranteed to find something for the whole family.
